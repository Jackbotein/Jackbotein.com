About
Beaver Country Day Student Council is a group of students, elected by their classmates, who are concerned with the well-being of their school. They span the 9th, 10th, 11th and 12th grades, with each grading sending three class representatives to Student Council. Additionally, the student body elects a President, Vice President, Treasurer, Director of Communications and Representative to the Board of Trustees. The core purpose of student council is to organize social events and work on solving problems that arise during the school year. As the name suggest, Student Council is composed of Beaver students, and our fundamental goal is to create a great high school experience for all students.
