BVR | Speaker Series

We are so excited to announce to the formation of the BVR | Speaker Series, where (as the name suggests) Beaver students will hear from several legendary individuals.

BVR | Speaker Series will allow students to hear from athletes, artists, activists, CEO’s and politicians who are truly leading our society. Our first speaker will be Bill Belichick, Head Coach of the New England Patriots. Coach Belichick has led the Patriots to Six Super Bowl Victories, a testiment to his coaching excellence.

If you have any questions about the BVR | Speaker Series feel free to reach out to lead organizer Jack Botein '23 at boteinj23@bcdschool.org.
