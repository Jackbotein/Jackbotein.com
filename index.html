Food Truck Fridays

1. Once per sports season, we bring two food trucks on to campus (think ROXY's + Stoked Pizza)

2. Sports games will run in conjunction w Food Truck Friday, building community and boosting game turnout

3. To decide which food trucks we bring on campus, we'll run a poll

Beaver Cares: Designing for a Cause

1. All 10th grade advisories create designs for tshirt, incorportating the mission of a nonprofit of their choice (ex. Boston Medical Center, Rosie's Place, Greater Boston Food Bank)

2. Designs are submitted to impartial Beaver teacher, who chooses their favorite design

3. The winning design is printed onto tshirts, and sold online and @ class meeting

4. 100% of proceeds go to nonprofit on the tshirt
