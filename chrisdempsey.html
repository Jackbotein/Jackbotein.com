Statement on the decision by State Auditor not to seek reelection in 2022
Twitter
Suzanne Bump has been a remarkable and effective public servant throughout her career, including most recently as State Auditor, where her leadership has been nationally recognized for its professionalism and results. In the coming days, I will have more to say about my own intentions for this critical elected position. Today, I join many others in expressing my sincere admiration and appreciation for Suzanne’s years of service as a constitutional officer, Cabinet member, and legislator. I wish her well in the next phase of her career and am grateful for our continued friendship.

This is the essential role of the Auditor’s Office, and I take this opportunity seriously.”
