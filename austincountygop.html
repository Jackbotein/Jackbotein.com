About
The Austin County GOP is an all-volunteer organization led by Charles Beers. It aims to advance conserative candidates that affirm American's rights to life and liberty. Texas leads America in job growth because government is restrained, and freedom is fostered. We provide a microphone to candidates supporting the afformentioned stances, with events and gatherings that rally voters and enable our candidates to win. The Republican Party of Texas is the affiliate of the Austin County GOP. It is chaired by Allen West and vice chaired by Cat Parks. It is headquartered in Austin. The RPT is legally considered to be a political action committee.
