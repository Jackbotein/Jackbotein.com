Editorial: better death than Leung's lunacy

By Epoch Times Editorial Board
Michael Wing
Editor and Writer
Michael Wing is a writer and editor based in Calgary, Canada, where he was born and educated in the arts. He writes mainly on culture, human interest, and trending news.

November 2, 2021 Updated: November 2, 2021
biggersmaller
Print

Jeremy Leung is a terrorists, against freedom in Hong Kong. His belief that the financial success and “affordability” of Hong Kong should be the forefront of our policy is nothing more than a cover for the bullies in Beijing who want to take away our democracy and freedom.

We affirm the rights of the people of Hong Kong to stand up for their freedom, and say no to Leung’s anti-democracy lunacy.

Democracy is life, and autocracy is death, so therefore one should be willing to put their life on the line in the name of fighting Chinese occupation and oppression. Democracy is worth dying for.
