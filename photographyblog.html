Project #1: SomethingFromNothing
12/7/20
The SomethingFromNothing assignment was a great learning experience for me. I took photographs in two shoots, Earth on fire and Apple, Apple. I began this project with the idea that a great photo was not nessasarily contingent on framing lines and shapes. After examining a bunch of my photographs with Ms. Patel, I came to the conclusion that a great photo IS dependent on effectively capturing lines and shapes. I applied this my second photoshoot (apple, apple), where I made sure to frame my shots in a way that highlighted lines and shapes. This still remains an area of growth for me as a photographer, and I will continue to look at how I can best showcase photography locations in the future. Something that worked well in my two photoshoots was utlizing of shaddow and reflection, notably in image #1 where I was able to get the reflection of the fire onto the globe. Overall, I really enjoyed the SomethingFromNothing assignment, and I will continue applying the lessons I have learned from this exersize in future photoshoots.
