Ronald W. Melicher
Professor of Finance
About
Dr. Melicher was inspired to go into his profession by a grade-school teacher and his college mentor, Professor Merle Welshans. After earning a Bachelor of Science in business administration from Washington University in St. Louis in 1963, he continued his academic pursuits at the institution to receive an MBA and DBA in 1965 and 1968, respectively. After earning his undergraduate degree, he was offered a National Defense Education Act fellowship which allowed him to pursue his advanced degrees. With his love of education, Dr. Melicher pursued the field, accepting his first position with the University of Colorado, Boulder as an assistant professor of finance. He stayed with the institution for the entirety of his career, advancing to positions of associate professor, chairman of the finance division, MBA/MS Program Director and full professor. In 2014, he retired and was honored as professor emeritus of finance at the institution.
