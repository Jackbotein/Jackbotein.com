Lindsey B. Abdalla is an attorney providing legal services covering Family Law.

Lindsey Abdalla, who practices law in Summit, Mississippi, was selected to Rising Stars for 2014 - 2020. This peer designation is awarded only to a select number of accomplished attorneys in each state. The Rising Stars selection process takes into account peer recognition, professional achievement in legal practice, and other cogent factors.

Prior to becoming an attorney, she studied at Mississippi College School of Law. She graduated in 2009. After passing the bar exam, she was admitted to legal practice in 2010.
